# Overview

Broad, cross-service overviews of Google Cloud's data and AI capabilities. Each subfolder covers a theme with standalone notebooks that survey the landscape for that topic.

## Contents

- **[ML Training & Batch Inference](ml-training/)** — Standalone notebooks demonstrating ML training and batch inference across BigQuery ML, BigFrames, Dataproc Serverless, Vertex AI Training, Colab Enterprise, Model Registry, and KFP orchestration. All notebooks share one BigQuery dataset for direct comparison.
